Glass Guide

Toughened Glass
Kya Hota Hai?

Toughened glass ek high-strength glass hota hai jo normal glass se 4-5 guna zyada strong hota hai. Yeh special heating process se banaya jata hai jisse yeh durable aur safe ban jata hai.

Yeh doors, partitions, railings aur shower areas me use hota hai. Agar toot ta bhi hai to chhote safe tukdo me toot ta hai jo injury ka risk kam karte hain.

Key Benefits

✓

High strength & long durability

✓

Safety glass (breaks into small pieces)

✓

Heat resistant & reliable

Aluminium vs Wooden Windows

Dono options ke apne benefits hain, lekin modern homes me aluminium windows zyada preferred hain.

Aluminium Windows

  • ✔ Durable & long-lasting
  • ✔ Low maintenance
  • ✔ Rust-free material
  • ✔ Modern & sleek design

Wooden Windows

  • ✔ Traditional & classic look
  • ✖ High maintenance required
  • ✖ Termite issues possible
  • ✖ Less durable over time
